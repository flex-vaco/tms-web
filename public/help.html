TMS — User Manual

Smart Timesheet Management for teams. This guide covers all features across Employee, Manager, and Admin roles. Please use the feedback form at the bottom to share your experience.

Version 1.0 Beta
Status UAT / Testing
Date March 2026
📋

Application Overview

All Roles

TMS is a web-based timesheet management system. It allows employees to record daily work hours per project, managers to review and approve timesheets, and administrators to manage the entire platform.

RoleWhat you can do
EmployeeLog hours daily, submit timesheets for approval, view own reports, receive notifications
ManagerEverything an Employee can do, plus: review & approve/reject team timesheets, view team reports
AdminEverything a Manager can do, plus: manage users, projects, org settings, holidays
ℹ️
The application runs at http://localhost (port 80) for the web UI and http://localhost:3001 for the API. All data is scoped to your organisation — users from different organisations cannot see each other's data.
🔐

Logging In

All Roles

The login page is the entry point for all users. Sessions persist across browser refreshes using a secure cookie.

1
Navigate to the app URL in your browser. You will be redirected to the Login page automatically.
2
Enter your Email address and Password.
3
Click Sign in. You will be taken to the Dashboard.
4
To log out, click your name/avatar in the top-right corner and select Logout. Your session is fully cleared.
💡
Your session is automatically restored when you return to the app or refresh the page — you will not need to log in again as long as your session (7 days) is still valid.
🏠

Dashboard

All Roles

The Dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your timesheet activity. The content adapts to your role.

This Week Hours
Total hours logged in the current calendar week
This Month Hours
Cumulative hours logged in the current month
Billable %
Percentage of your logged hours marked as billable
Pending Approvals
Number of timesheets awaiting manager review (Managers see team count)

Quick Actions provide shortcuts to the most common tasks:

  • New Timesheet — opens the Timesheet page for the current week
  • Copy Last Week — creates a new draft pre-filled with last week's project rows
  • Export Report — navigates to Reports page
  • Settings — navigates to Admin Settings (Admin only)

Recent Timesheets lists your last few submitted/approved timesheets with their status badges:

● Draft ● Submitted ● Approved ● Rejected

Timesheet Entry

All Roles

The Timesheet page is where you record hours worked each day, per project. One timesheet covers one calendar week (Mon–Sun).

Navigating Weeks

1
Use the ← → arrows in the week navigator to move between weeks. The current week is shown by default.
2
The week range (e.g., Mon 3 Mar – Sun 9 Mar) is displayed in the centre of the navigator bar.

Adding Time Entries

1
Click + Add Row (or the project selector) to add a new project row to the timesheet grid.
2
Select a Project from the dropdown. Only projects you are assigned to will appear.
3
Enter hours for each day (Mon–Sun) in the numeric cells. Hours are entered as decimals (e.g., 1.5 = 1 hour 30 mins).
4
Optionally add a description for each day to describe what you worked on.
5
Use the Billable toggle to mark whether the hours are billable to a client. This is per-row (per project).
6
The row total and the Summary Bar (Total / Billable / Non-Billable) update automatically.
ℹ️
Time-off field: Each day also has an optional Time Off field (e.g., sick leave, annual leave). This is tracked separately from work hours and appears in the monthly export.

Copy Previous Week

1
Click Copy Previous Week in the week navigator bar.
2
The system finds your most recent approved or submitted week and copies the project rows (without hours or descriptions) into a new draft for the selected week.
3
If a draft already exists for that week, you will be prompted to confirm overwriting it.
⚠️
Hours are not copied — only the project structure is. You must enter hours for each day manually.
📤

Submitting a Timesheet

All Roles

Once you have entered all hours for the week, you submit the timesheet for manager approval.

1
Verify your hours in the Summary Bar at the top of the timesheet page.
2
Click the Submit button in the top-right of the week navigator.
3
The timesheet status changes from Draft to Submitted. The grid becomes read-only.
4
The assigned manager receives a notification to review your submission.
🚫
Submitted timesheets cannot be edited. If you need to make corrections after submission, ask your manager to reject it — you can then edit and re-submit it.

After Rejection

1
You will receive a notification that your timesheet was rejected, along with the reason provided by the manager.
2
The timesheet returns to an editable state. Make your corrections.
3
Click Submit again to re-send for approval.
🔔

Notifications

All Roles

The bell icon in the top navigation bar shows your unread notification count. Notifications are generated for key events.

EventWho receives it
Timesheet submittedAssigned manager
Timesheet approvedEmployee who submitted
Timesheet rejectedEmployee who submitted (includes reason)
Timesheet due reminderAll employees (if configured by Admin)
1
Click the 🔔 bell icon to open the notification panel.
2
Click a notification to mark it as read, or use Mark all as read.

Approvals

ManagerAdmin

The Approvals page lists all timesheets submitted by your direct reports that are awaiting review.

ℹ️
Managers only see timesheets from their direct reports. Admins can see all submitted timesheets in the organisation.

Approving a Timesheet

1
Navigate to Approvals from the sidebar. The stat bar shows pending count, team hours, and team members.
2
Review the timesheet card: employee name, week range, total hours, billable hours.
3
Click Details to see the full breakdown of daily hours and project entries.
4
Click Approve to mark the timesheet as approved. The employee is notified immediately.

Rejecting a Timesheet

1
Click Reject on the timesheet card.
2
A dialog will prompt you to enter a rejection reason. This is required and will be sent to the employee.
3
Click Confirm Reject. The timesheet returns to the employee for correction.
🚫
Self-approval is not allowed. A manager cannot approve their own timesheet. Another manager or an admin must approve it.
📊

Reports

ManagerAdmin

The Reports page lets you generate and export timesheet data with flexible filters.

Generating a Report

1
Select a date range using the preset (e.g., This Month, Last Month) or pick custom From/To dates.
2
Optionally filter by Employee, Status (Draft/Submitted/Approved/Rejected), or Project.
3
Click Generate. The stat bar and chart update to show aggregated results.

Exporting Data

FormatBest for
📊 ExcelAnalysing data in spreadsheets; styled with summary row
📄 PDFSharing formatted reports; landscape layout
📋 CSVImporting into other systems or databases
📅 Monthly TimesheetDay-by-day Excel export per employee per month (includes holidays & time-off)
ℹ️
Employees can export their own data only. Managers see their direct reports' data. Admins see all organisation data.

Admin — Users & Roles

Admin

Manage all user accounts in your organisation from Admin → Users & Roles.

Creating a User

1
Click + Add User. A modal dialog opens.
2
Fill in Name, Email, Role (Employee / Manager / Admin), Department (optional), and an initial Password.
3
Click Save. The user can now log in immediately.

Editing & Deactivating Users

1
Click the edit (pencil) icon on a user row to update their name, role, department, or status.
2
To deactivate a user (e.g., when they leave the company), click the deactivate (trash) icon. The user is set to Inactive and cannot log in — their historical data is preserved.
⚠️
Users are never permanently deleted — they are deactivated. This preserves historical timesheet data for reporting.
📁

Admin — Projects

Admin

Projects are what employees log time against. Each project has a unique code and belongs to a client.

Creating a Project

1
Go to Admin → Projects and click + Add Project.
2
Enter the project Code (e.g., PRJ-2024-001), Name, Client, and Budget Hours.
3
Assign Managers and Employees who will work on this project. Only assigned employees will see the project in their timesheet dropdown.
4
Click Save.
Budget Hours
Target total hours for the project. Used to calculate utilisation in reports.
Used Hours
Auto-calculated from approved time entries. Updated when timesheets are approved.
⚙️

Admin — Holidays

Admin

Define public holidays for your organisation. Holidays are visible to all users and are highlighted in the monthly timesheet export.

1
Go to Admin → Holidays and click + Add Holiday.
2
Enter the holiday Name and Date.
3
Toggle Recurring if this holiday repeats every year (e.g., Christmas Day).
4
Click Save.

Testing Checklist

All Roles

Please work through the following scenarios during your testing session and note any issues in the feedback form below.

Employee Scenarios

  • ☐Log in with the Employee account and navigate to the Dashboard.
  • ☐Open the Timesheet page. Verify the correct week is shown.
  • ☐Add at least two project rows and enter hours for multiple days.
  • ☐Toggle billable/non-billable on one row and verify the Summary Bar updates.
  • ☐Add a per-day description to at least one entry.
  • ☐Save the timesheet as a draft. Navigate away and return — verify data is persisted.
  • ☐Submit the timesheet. Verify it becomes read-only.
  • ☐Use "Copy Previous Week" for a new week — verify hours are not copied but projects are.
  • ☐Check the Notifications panel for the submission acknowledgement.

Manager Scenarios

  • ☐Log in with the Manager account. Verify Approvals appears in the sidebar.
  • ☐Navigate to Approvals. Verify pending timesheet from Employee is visible.
  • ☐Click Details to review the full timesheet breakdown.
  • ☐Approve the timesheet. Verify it disappears from the queue.
  • ☐Submit your own timesheet (as Manager is also Employee). Verify you cannot approve it yourself.
  • ☐Reject a timesheet with a reason. Log in as Employee to verify the rejection notification and reason.
  • ☐Generate a report for the current month and export as Excel.

Admin Scenarios

  • ☐Log in with the Admin account. Verify Admin panel appears in the sidebar.
  • ☐Create a new user with the Employee role. Log in as that user to verify access.
  • ☐Create a new project and assign the employee to it. Verify the employee sees it in their timesheet.
  • ☐Add a public holiday. Verify it appears in the holidays list.
  • ☐Update organisation settings (e.g., change Max Hours/Day to 10) and verify it is saved.
  • ☐Deactivate a user and verify they can no longer log in.
  • ☐Generate a Monthly Timesheet export and verify holidays are highlighted.

Known Behaviour & Limitations

All Roles

The following are expected behaviours during this testing phase — these are not bugs:

  • ℹ️
    Email notifications are not yet active. Notification reminders appear only in-app. Email delivery (SendGrid/SES) is pending configuration.
  • ℹ️
    Integrations tab is a placeholder. Payroll and PM system integrations are listed in Admin but are not yet connected.
  • ℹ️
    Charts are placeholders. The Hours Breakdown chart on the Reports page uses static placeholder UI — real Recharts integration is pending.
  • ℹ️
    Forgot password link is not functional. The "Forgot password?" link on the login page is UI-only. Password resets require an admin to update via the Users panel.
  • ℹ️
    Session timeout is 15 minutes of inactivity (access token lifetime). The app will silently refresh your session in the background using the secure cookie. You should not be interrupted.
